List of Websites that can help any Creator!

List of websites that can give you high-quality photos, videos, and audio for completely free and free to use for personal or commercial use, under their Licenses. The list of websites below is My personal use for any project that I create and I use it for any social media platform.

List of Websites for Creators

December 16, 2022 by AlexanderZone.net

PEXELS

Pexels provides high-quality and completely free stock photos and videos licensed under the Pexels license. All photos are nicely tagged, searchable, and also easy to discover through our discover pages. Pexels is run by Bruno Joseph, Ingo Joseph, and Daniel Frese. Bruno and Ingo co-founded Pexels together in 2014 and Daniel joined them in 2015. Pexels help millions of designers, writers, artists, programmers, and other creators to get access to beautiful photos that they can use freely which empowers them to create amazing products, designs, stories, websites, apps, art, and other work.

Visit the website here: www.pexels.com

PRODUCTIONCRATE

This website is perfect for video editors and VFX artists, this website provides any VFX artist and Video Editor Needs like Visual Effects, Sound Effects, Backgrounds, Graphics, 3d Models, extensions and so much more. You can use it for personal and commercial use but you are required if possible to mention their website for the credit and to gain more users to their website. They offer free membership (5 downloads per day) and a paid membership.
The only problem that you may notice is if you use a free account, you can only have 5 downloads per day.

Visit the website here: www.productioncrate.com

ZAPSPLAT

This website provides you with a large amount of pure audio files, whether sound effects or full music for a project. You can download and use the audio for your personal use or commercial use. Also, you can use a free basic account, or a paid account (Gold member). Free accounts have limited downloads per day and they can only download a .mp3 file they are also required if possible to mention their website for credits. While the gold members have the option to download a .wav file and did not required to mention their website for use.

Visit the website here: www.zapsplat.com

YOUTUBE AUDIO LIBRARY (Inside Youtube Studio)

If you are a Vlogger, especially a Youtuber, you probably know that youtube provides you with an audio library for you to use on any videos that you upload on youtube and no attributions are required. This is perfect for a YouTuber who starting recently and doesn’t have any resources to purchase audio for the youtube channel.

Visit the website here: You need a Youtube Channel.
